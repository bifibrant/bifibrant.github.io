Scott Balchin

Postdoctoral fellow at the MPIM Bonn

Bifibrant Administrative Home

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Max Planck Institute of Mathematics in Bonn, mainly interested in all things related to tensor-triangulated geometry and Quillen model categories. My mentor is Tobias Barthel.

I completed my Ph.D. at the University of Leicester in 2017 under the supervision of Frank Neumann. Following this I was a postdoc at the University of Sheffield and the University of Warwick with John Greenlees working on the project "Adelic models, rigidity and equivariant cohomology".

email: balchin@mpim-bonn.mpg.de

Latest updates

  • I will be giving a 5 part lecture series on Quillen model categories in November in conjunction with the Transpennine Topology Triangle network. Details to follow!
  • My book project A Handbook of Model Categories has been completed and is now with referees. The next step for the project is setting up a Stacks Project style interface along with a database of examples which will go under the name Bifibrant. Please feel free to get in touch with me if you have an example you strongly feel should make an appearance.
  • I started a new postdoc position at the MPIM Bonn.
  • My joint paper with John Greenlees "Adelic models of tensor-triangulated categories" has been published in Advances in Mathematics.
